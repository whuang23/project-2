Intro to HFAI

Established in 2004, The Automaton Institute robot club from Affiliated High School of SCNU (South China Normal University) aimed at advance technology development. Thanks for constructions of several years' students, the AI robot club owns biggest activity area all over the school now and over 20 members as well.

Our target are developing students' interests of robots and skills of physical jobs as well as innovations.

Also, we not only study for the robot knowledge formally but also learn how to play with robots.

In the school organizations exhibition day, we exhibited oceans of innovation robots with its funny structure like walking, obstacle avoidance and Automatic Intelligence robots soccer games. You can flight with AI robots with a game controller. But the most interesting thing is writing the “smart” program as a AI programmer in order to make AI robots win the game all the time! Besides, we participate in many competitions in our city and province even the whole country ,what's more, The Robo Cup Junior(RCJ). As a school team member, you can win the world wild prize only if you do well in our club.

If you already have strong ability of engineering or programming, come and join us as soon as possible. We have made you a sky to fly!

New students are welcomed as well, you will learn something you can't get from text book in practice and rigorous way to think.

Join us, and seize the opportunity to realize your aspiration.
